## What are well-structured prompts?

A well-structured prompt is not a simple request. It is a **communication blueprint** between the human and the AI.

### Core elements of an effective prompt

- AI role  
- Clear objective  
- Business context  
- Rules and constraints  
- Output structure  
- Quality criteria  

While a simple prompt says â€œwrite an articleâ€, a structured prompt defines **what to write, for whom, at what depth, and for what purpose**.
